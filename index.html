About me

Hello! My name is Ayman Asli, a computer engineering student and passionate about software development.
As a young self-taught individual, I've acquired a broad range of computer skills, notably in automation. I am convinced that everything can be automated to save time and improve efficiency, which is why I chose to pursue my career in this field.
Since my admission to CESI, a renowned engineering school, I've been exposed to a variety of stimulating projects. I've implemented a corporate network infrastructure with Pfsense and connections between different sites (Lyon and Rouen). I also developed a software that performs backup tasks in C#, and I carried out a big data project that does data extraction with Talend and Hive.
Outside of computing, I am a great sports enthusiast, particularly volleyball and bodybuilding. I firmly believe that sport is essential for maintaining a healthy mind in a healthy body.
In the short term, I hope to undertake more missions, and in the long term, I wish to improve my level on Ansible and Kubernetes in order to automate the deployment of Rancher. Every day, I work with passion, motivation and a head full of ideas to reach my goals.
